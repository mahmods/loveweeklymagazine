After being hauled off by government agents and detained for questioning after possessing a large amount of cash, one Brit made headlines when he proved them wrong by showing them how he had amassed his wealth. In fact the gentleman had just appeared on Dragon Den and struck the largest deal EVER! The idea that struck him that deal : it was simple, he created a program that allows any average person to make cash through the bitcoin boom regardless of their previous history in investing or technology.

A user would simply make an initial deposit into the platform, usually of £180 (or $250, as the platform works with USD) or more, and the automated trading algorithm would go to work. Using a combination of data and machine learning, the algorithm would know the perfect time to buy Bitcoin low and sell high, maximising the user's profit.

The entrepreneurs intend to make money from their platform by charging a small commission only on the profits a user generates. They asked the Dragons for £80,000 for 25% of the company, valuing the company at £320,000.

To demonstrate the power of their company's platform the entrepreneurs had Deborah Meaden deposit £180 live on the show.
